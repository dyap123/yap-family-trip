Track every expense for the trip. Real-time sync between Danzel's phone and yours via Firebase. 所有支出实时同步。

Tap the gold + floating button (bottom-right). Form opens.

2

Date, description, amount in USD, who paid (chip-pick). Use Today for the date if it's right now.

3

Four big buttons: Everyone · 所有人, Group 1 · 少年组, Group 2 · 家人组, or Pick people · 选人. The amount divides evenly across whoever's in the selected group.

4

The Exclude · 排除 picker below the split mode. Tap any names to remove them from the share. E.g., "Everyone − Eugene" if Eugene already left.

Editing the plan修改行程

PAGE 3 / 4

AHotels · 酒店

On the Trip · 行程 tab, every hotel card shows a yellow pencil only when you're in manager mode. Tap it to edit:

  • Name · 名称 — fix typos or rename
  • Dates / nights · 日期 — adjust check-in / out
  • City · 城市, Who · 谁, Notes · 备注
  • Lat / Lng · 经纬度 — moves the pin on the map
  • Map URL · 地图链接 — what the "Open Maps" button opens

Edits save to Firebase as overrides. Family members see your changes within ~30s. Reset · 还原 button removes the override and reverts to the original.

BMap navigation · 地图导航

Map tab has 3 things you should know about:

  • Tour 13 days · 自动游览 — auto-plays through every hotel + activity, ~2.4s per day. Stops on tab change.
  • Day strip below the map — tap any date to fly the map to that day's location. Auto-scrolls to keep the active day in view.
  • Today's pin pulses yellow with a 「今天 · TODAY」 badge on top.

CTrip view · 列表 / 日历

On the Trip tab, toggle between List · 列表 (scroll through 13 cards) and Calendar · 日历 (overview grid). Tap any day in calendar view to jump to its full card.

Quick tips小提示

PAGE 4 / 4

★Rules of thumb · 经验

  • Lock manager mode when handing your phone to anyone. Tap the lock-open icon in the header to drop manager.
  • Switching profiles via Settings → "Switch user · 切换" auto-revokes manager mode if the new profile isn't Danzel/Angel.
  • Sync test — add an expense on your phone, check Danzel's phone shows it within ~5s. If not, his Firebase URL might not be set on his device (Money tab → Connect Firebase).
  • Edits don't appear? Pull-to-refresh on Safari, or Settings → Reload data. The 60-second cache may also be stale.

⚙Behind the scenes · 系统

  • Schedule + food list live in the Google Sheet (read-only).
  • Expenses + edit-overrides live in Firebase (real-time).
  • Ah Zai talks to MiniMax directly from the browser.
  • The whole app is a single HTML file — open dev tools to see it.
